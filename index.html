Who We Are

A Trusted Partner in Elder Care

Care Connect is a referral agency based in Washington State dedicated to bridging the gap between seniors seeking safe, comfortable homes and the caregiving professionals ready to serve them. We work closely with families, care providers, and facilities to ensure every match is the right one.

💛

Compassionate

We treat every senior and family with the care and dignity they deserve throughout the entire process.

🔍

Thorough

We carefully evaluate homes and caregivers to ensure quality, safety, and the right fit every time.

🤲

Community-Focused

Rooted in Washington, we're committed to strengthening local elder care communities statewide.

What We Do

Our Services

🏡

Senior Home Placement

We help elderly individuals and their families navigate the process of finding the right care home — from adult family homes to assisted living facilities across Washington State. Our referrals are personalized, free for families, and focused on quality of life.

👩‍⚕️

Caregiver Job Placement

Are you a caregiver looking for work? We connect qualified caregiving professionals with homes and facilities that need their skills. Whether you're experienced or just starting out, we help you find a role that fits your schedule and strengths.

📋

Provider Partnerships

Are you a care home owner or facility provider in Washington? Partner with Care Connect to receive quality referrals. Reach out to us directly by phone or email to discuss how we can work together to serve more seniors in our community.
